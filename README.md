# PawBloom

PawBloom is a bilingual pet diary and care-record app built around one idea: pets should be able to grow, be loved, and bloom beautifully through the records their family keeps.

The app helps caregivers capture daily memories, health observations, care routines, medication notes, and vet-ready briefings so a pet's story is not scattered across chats, photos, and memory.

PawBloom means blooming with care. The product emphasizes steady, warm record keeping that helps every pet's life and growth become visible over time.
